Why ReefTrack?

Coral reefs are among the most biodiverse ecosystems on the planet, yet they face unprecedented threats from climate change, pollution, and overfishing. ReefTrack provides a centralized platform for collecting, managing, and analyzing coral reef data.

  • Standardized data collection for consistent monitoring
  • Role-based access control for data quality assurance
  • Approval workflow ensuring verified contributions
  • Real-time dashboard for monitoring reef health status
