About ReefTrack

Empowering coral reef conservation through technology and collaboration

Our Mission

ReefTrack is a web-based coral reef assessment and monitoring information system designed to support researchers, conservationists, and communities in tracking the health of coral reef ecosystems.

By providing a centralized platform for data collection, management, and analysis, we aim to streamline the process of coral reef monitoring and enable data-driven decision-making for reef conservation efforts.

What We Offer

Comprehensive tools for coral reef monitoring and assessment

Geospatial Mapping

Visualize coral reef locations on interactive maps, enabling spatial analysis and geographic tracking of reef health across regions.

Time-Series Analytics

Track changes in reef health over time with powerful analytics, identifying trends and patterns that inform conservation strategies.

Data Management

Store and organize reef monitoring data with standardized formats, ensuring consistency and reliability across all submissions.

Quality Assurance

Built-in approval workflows ensure that all data contributions are reviewed and verified before being added to the system.
